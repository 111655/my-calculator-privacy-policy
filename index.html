Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 09 July 2026

Your privacy matters. My Calculator works completely offline and does not collect or share your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

My Calculator does not collect, store, or transmit personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, contacts, location, or photos.

2. Local Data Storage

The following data is stored only on your device:

  • Calculation History
  • Favorite Calculations
  • Memory Values
  • Theme Preferences
  • Application Settings

3. Permissions

Permissions requested by the application are used only to support features such as:

  • Sharing calculations
  • Exporting history

4. Data Sharing

We never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. All calculator data remains stored locally on your device.

5. Third-Party Services

My Calculator currently does not use:

  • Advertising SDKs
  • Analytics Services
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Crash Reporting Services

6. Children's Privacy

My Calculator does not knowingly collect information from children and is safe for general audiences.

7. Security

All your calculations and settings remain stored securely on your device. We never have access to your information.

8. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be published on this page.
