Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 09 July 2026

Thank you for using My Calculator. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled.

1. Information We Collect

My Calculator does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information such as:

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Location
  • Contacts
  • Photos

The application works completely offline for its calculator features.

2. Local Data Storage

The following information is stored only on your device:

  • Calculation History
  • Favorite Calculations
  • Memory Values
  • Theme Preferences
  • Application Settings

This information never leaves your device.

3. Permissions

The app may request permissions required for features such as:

  • Sharing calculations
  • Exporting calculation history

These permissions are used only for the requested functionality.

4. Data Sharing

We do not:

  • Sell your data
  • Share your information with third parties
  • Upload your calculations to our servers

5. Third-Party Services

Currently, My Calculator does not use:

  • Advertising SDKs
  • Analytics Services
  • Firebase
  • Crash Reporting Services

If such services are added in future updates, this Privacy Policy will be updated.

6. Children's Privacy

My Calculator is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Security

All calculations and settings remain stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this information.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
